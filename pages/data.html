Data

We planned to use part of the dataset found in Kaggle, by filtering those Hip-Hop songs. There are around 380,000+ lyrics in the original data set, and there will be about thousands Hip-Hop songs among them.

The structure is:

[index]: the unique integer to tag each song, will be reassigned after filtering;

[song]: string, the song’s name, words are connected by “-”;

[year]: integer, the year when the song was released;

[artist]: string, the artist’s name, words are connected by “-”;

[genre]: string, equals “Hip-Hop” after filtering;

[lyrics]: string, the lyrics of each song, sepearted by lines, empty value will be deleted from the filtered dataset.
